Password Do's and Don'ts

❌ Common Mistakes

  • Using personal information
  • Short passwords (under 12 chars)
  • No special characters
  • Password reuse across sites

✅ Best Practices

  • Minimum 12 characters
  • Mix of upper/lower case
  • Numbers and symbols
  • Unique for each account
